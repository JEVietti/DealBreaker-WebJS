Find Your Match!

Each person lists their interests and what they are looking for in their potential matches. However, each profile also contains their deal breakers, to help make a better and quicker descision.

Community Feedback

Users are able to give feedback on each other by commenting on the user's profile, available for other potential matches to see. This is not a forum for bullying, but will help prevent the creation of malicious profiles.

Quick and Easy

Simply browse for users to find your potential match, or navigate to their profile by username. Once added, you can set up dates through messages or exchange contact information and keep it completely private.



Free and Open

Dealbreaker is a free and open source dating website, supported by the user's choice to donate.
